My Hobbies

Things that bring me joy

Painting

I love creating beautiful artwork, especially custom scripture art for clients. From sketching to final touches, painting is my creative outlet where I can express emotion and storytelling through colors and brushstrokes.

Watching Hockey

I'm a huge hockey fan and love supporting the Utah Mammoth! There's nothing quite like the energy and excitement of a live game. Whether I'm cheering from the stands or watching with friends, hockey brings so much joy and adrenaline to my life.

Getting a Diet Coke

One of my favorite little treats is grabbing a cold Diet Coke. It’s the perfect pick-me-up whether I’m cheering on the Utah Mammoth, hanging out with friends, or just enjoying a quiet moment.

Doing Nails

As a nail technician, I get to transform nails into miniature works of art. Whether it's intricate designs, vibrant colors, or elegant finishes, creating beautiful nails for my clients brings so much satisfaction and joy to my days.

Hangout with Family

Spending quality time with my loved ones is everything to me. Whether we're laughing together, supporting each other, or just enjoying each other's company, these moments are the most precious and memorable.

Traveling

Exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, and discovering hidden gems around the world fuels my adventurous spirit. From my mission in Chile to dream destinations, travel opens my heart and mind to endless possibilities.
